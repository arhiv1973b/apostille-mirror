## LEGAL QUALIFICATION

### Rome Statute, Article 7 — Crimes Against Humanity

> "Crimes against humanity" means any of the following acts when committed as part of a widespread or systematic attack directed against any civilian population, with knowledge of the attack:
>
> - Murder
> - Extermination
> - Enslavement
> - Deportation
> - Imprisonment
> - Torture
> - Sexual violence
> - Persecution
> - Enforced disappearance

### ECHR Article 3

- Prohibition of torture and inhuman treatment
- Right to life (procedural obligation to investigate)
