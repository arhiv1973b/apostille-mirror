### Jus Cogens
The prohibition of torture is a peremptory norm (jus cogens) under international law (Vienna Convention Art. 53). All derivative proceedings are void ab initio as they are based on evidence obtained through torture ("poisoned fruit" doctrine).

### Continuing Breach
The Moldovan courts' decisions constitute a continuing international wrong, engaging state responsibility under international law.

### Erga Omnes
Obligation to investigate exists erga omnes (ICJ, Barcelona Traction, 1970).
