## Summary of Evidence

### Critical Finding: Art. 5 Hague Convention Violation

**13 apostilles (14.4% of 90)** were rendered defective through the **deliberate omission of the printed signatory name** in Field 2 — a mandatory identification field under Art. 5 of the Hague Convention 1961.

> The signature physically exists. The **printed surname/name (расшифровка подписи)** was intentionally not entered.  
> This is not an administrative error — it is a pattern across 13 paid documents.

### Context: Victim Status Concealed

These defective apostilles were issued to **a person whose status as a torture victim was actively concealed** by state authorities. The Plaintiff was denied rehabilitation in violation of Jus Cogens obligations (Art. 3 ECHR). The defectiveness of the apostilles is therefore not coincidental — it represents a continuation of the same institutional pattern.
