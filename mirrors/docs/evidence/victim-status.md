## Jus Cogens Dimension

The prohibition of torture is a **peremptory norm (jus cogens)** — it:
- Cannot be derogated from by any state act
- Generates **erga omnes** obligations (owed to the international community)
- Is **not subject to statutes of limitation** (Rome Statute Art. 29)
- Requires active investigation and rehabilitation — not merely passive acknowledgment

The state's failure to rehabilitate the Plaintiff — and its active concealment of his victim status — constitutes a continuing violation of these absolute obligations.
